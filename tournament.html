Full Tournament Rules

Welcome to The Madness of Billiards

Today, I want to invite you to participate in our first tournament with exceptional prizes, ranging from a $300 USD Amazon gift card to a $1,600 USD Apple gift card.

Here are the rules:

In multiplayer ranking mode, every match you win or lose will affect your points and determine your position in the global ranking.

In each match, you must bring three minions. Each minion has unique abilities, and finding the perfect combination will be key to your victory. Additionally, select a Cue that gives you an advantage with each shot and an Avatar that represents you.

Once in the match, there are four ways to win:

  1. Pocket all your assigned balls, leaving the 8-ball for last.
  2. Wait for your opponent to pocket the 8-ball prematurely.
  3. And the most strategic! Protect your heart's health. On each turn, you'll see stones blocking the pockets, and from each pocket, a minion of your opponent will emerge to attack your heart. If your heart's health reaches 0, you’ll lose the match!
  4. If your opponent surrenders or abandons the match.

'The Madness of Billiards' Qualifying Tournament and Championship

Qualifying Tournament:

The qualifying tournament will begin on October 1 and end on December 16. The top 32 players with the highest scores will advance to the main championship, which will be broadcast live on Twitch. This championship will conclude on January 10, where the top 8 players will be determined and awarded prizes.

Tournament Structure and Prizes

Initial Classification:

The top 32 players with the highest scores will advance to the main championship.

Elimination Phase:

In the championship, players will compete in direct elimination rounds. In each round, half of the players will be eliminated until the championship winner is determined.

Positions 3 to 8 will be assigned based on the score obtained in the qualifying round. In the event of a tie, an additional match will be organized, which will be broadcast live, to determine the final position.

Prizes:

The 8 players who reach the final phase will receive prizes, with the highest positions receiving the most valuable rewards.

'The Madness of Billiards' Championship

Championship Start:

The championship will begin on December 18, with player matchups randomly determined and published on the game's social media. Matches will be played in friendly mode.

Tournament Format:

Matches:

Players will compete in a best-of-three format. If each player wins one match, a third match will be played to determine who advances to the next round.

Dates and Rounds:

  • December 18: Round of 32 players.
  • December 19: Round of 16 players.
  • December 20: Round of 8 players.
  • December 21: Semifinals and Final.

Participation Rules

Absences:

The absence of a player from their scheduled match will result in automatic disqualification, allowing their opponent to advance to the next round.

Schedule and Attendance:

Match schedules will be published for all players, who must be present 15 minutes before the scheduled time. Failure to attend at the scheduled time will result in disqualification.

Broadcasting:

All matches will be streamed live on Twitch. Players are not required to have a camera or microphone; they just need to be present and ready to play.

Prize Delivery Policies for "THE MADNESS OF BILLIARDS"

Special Conditions:

Friendly matches will not affect your score; only competitive matches will count towards your ranking.

Score Calculation:

Before each match, the probability of winning is calculated based on your current score and that of your opponent. If you win, your score will increase; if you lose, it will decrease.

Score Adjustment:

The number of points you gain or lose will depend on whether you won or lost, and the initial probability of winning. After each match, your score will be updated to reflect your performance in the tournament.
